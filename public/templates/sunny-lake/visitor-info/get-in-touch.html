Do I need to make a reservation for park entry?

No reservation is required for general entry into Sunny Lake. However, specific activities like guided tours and certain campgrounds do require reservations. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak season.

What is the best time to visit Sunny Lake?

Sunny Lake is beautiful year-round, but the best time to visit depends on what activities you’re interested in. Spring and summer are perfect for hiking, boating, and wildlife viewing, while fall offers stunning foliage and winter is ideal for snowshoeing and cozy cabin stays.

Where can I find information about weather and current conditions?

You can check current weather conditions and forecasts at the Visitor Centre or on our website’s ‘When to Go’ page. We also recommend dressing in layers and being prepared for sudden changes in weather.
