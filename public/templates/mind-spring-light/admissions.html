FAQ

In-Depth Responses to Your Questions

What are the school’s admission requirements?

To apply, you need to provide proof of age, recent academic records, and any other relevant documents depending on the grade level. For detailed requirements, please refer to our Admissions Criteria section.

What is the school’s curriculum focus?

Our curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive education with a focus on core subjects, including math, science, language arts, and social studies, as well as a variety of electives and extracurricular activities.

Are there programs for gifted and talented students?

Yes, we offer a range of specialized programs for gifted and talented students. These programs feature advanced coursework in core subjects, enrichment activities designed to challenge and engage students, and opportunities for independent research projects that allow students to explore their interests in depth.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a diverse array of extracurricular activities to enhance the student experience. These include competitive sports teams, music and drama clubs, student government, and a variety of special interest groups. Our aim is to provide students with opportunities to explore their passionsand develop new skills.

What are the school’s safety measures?

Our school prioritizes student safety with measures including secure campus access, regular safety drills, and a dedicated school safety team. We also have protocols in place for emergencies and health concerns.

What are the school hours and calendar?

Our typical school day runs from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. The school year follows a traditional academic calendar with breaks for holidays and vacations. For specific dates, please refer to our School Calendar.
